integrated technologies

We build on the Magento Platform, which provides extensive tools, flexibility and support in what we think will rapidly prove to the web’s most flexible ecommerce platform. But we also supplement Magento with industry leading tools, custom software development and open source applications to provide cost effective solutions tailored to meet each clients‘ needs. In doing so, we’ve pioneered the integration of Magento with these leading technologies:

  • endeca: search & guided navigation
  • cybersource: hosted payment (simple PCI compliance) + fraud check
  • coremetrics: robust site analytics
  • ATG recommendations engine

We’ve also developed the following custom Magento features during recent projects:

  • simplified checkout
  • enhanced customer database + exports
  • mass product attribute edits
  • mass product image uploads
  • firstData gift card integration
  • enhanced account features (move all to bag/Wishlist, email friend)
  • enhanced CSR features (edit order)
