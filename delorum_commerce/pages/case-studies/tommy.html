www.tommybahama.com

Tommy Bahama was midway through their first ecommerce site, over budget, and without a qualified Flash developer. We were contacted to take over Flash development, concept new site features and bring the budget back under control.

solution:

flash actionscript programming: We coded Tommy Bahama’s Flash to be modular, resulting in reusable features and lower development costs.

decoupled logic: By isolating backend logic from the frontend display, we enabled Tommy Bahama to implement seasonal design changes in just over an hour, without additional Flash development.

usability + user testing: We streamlined the checkout process and developed a gift bag option to enhance functionality and the user experience.

deliverables:

  • flash expertise
  • cost saving expansion
  • user design
  • market positioning
  • site admin tools
  • customer database
  • online coupons

the result:

Tommy Bahama met their launch and Flash devleopment came in under budget, and the site made 3 times their projected return within the first year. We’ve continued to work together to develop addtiional features for the site. We created detailed requirements documentation and architectural wireframes to help weigh benefits and long term ROI.
