www.zumiez.com

Zumiez had an aggressive ecommerce growth strategy, but highly unstable code base. They approached Delorum to implement a flexible new platform, customize for their needs, enhance front end usability, and integrate many of the leading ecommerce services... in half the time.

solution:

PCI Compliance: We began by architecting an environment and payment solution to reduce their PCI requirements from SAQ D to SAQ A, saving tens of thousands in policy, employment and auditing costs.

Frontend Redesign: While staying true to the Zumiez brand, we completely overhauled the user experience to simplify usability and increase functionality.

Administrative Tools: Working within the Magento™ platform, we provided robust, customized tools that streamlined the Zumiez workflow, giving complete catalog, customer service and promotion control to their in-house staff.

Best of Breed: We implemented some of the industry’s top solutions for guided navigation, analytics, recommendations and fraud screening into a single, seamless solution.

Ongoing Support: We continue to provide development services to Zumiez, working in tandem with their IT department and hosting provider to optimize site performance and execute a long-term strategy for growth.
