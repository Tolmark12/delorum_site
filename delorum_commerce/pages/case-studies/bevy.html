www.bevyinc.com

Seattle based Bevy approached us to help develop and brand a Facebook application devoted to women, fun and fashion. Our task was to develop a distinct user interface for a sophisticated range of functions.

solution:

Information Architecture: We created working development wireframes to help guide and map the evolution of technical features.

Targeted Brand: Research, competitive visual audits, design and user group testing all contributed to brand development.

User Experience: We developed a more contextual user experience by pioneering an expanded use of modal windows and Java within Facebook.

Corporate Site: A more sophisticated corporate image helped position Bevy for increased investment and expansion.

deliverables:

  • project architecture
  • design objectives
  • market positioning
  • facebook development

the result:

Bevy is one of the richest user experiences on Facebook to date. Just a few months after launch, Bevy boasts over 45,000 active users and has received unanimous 5-Star reviews from appreciative users.
